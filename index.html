About Me

I have worked on some of the country's largest congressional online fundraising campaigns. I am a highly disciplined and goal oriented change maker with a range of experience in full stack web development, digital marketing, data analytics, and teamwork. I am a critical thinker and I have a constant itch to learn more. I specialize in web development using the MERN Stack. I thrive off of utilizing data and technology to create beautiful and practical web pages.

I received a BA at West Virginia University in Political Science and my Masters of Public Administration from American University. From there, I spent four years on the campaign trail, working for Congressional and Mayoral campaigns in the field. While working on these campaigns, I fell in love with running the digital programs. While taking on these responsibilities, I gained new tools that led me to a digital fundraising firm where I worked for two years. And that's what led to my interest and pursuit of a career in web development at the University of Denver's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp!

When I'm not coding or reading the news, you can find me either on my bike, hiking in the mountains, or watching the Buffalo Bills (probably lose) on Sunday.
