I am a highly disciplined and goal oriented change maker with a range of experience in full stack web development, digital marketing, data analytics, and teamwork. I am a critical thinker and I have a constant itch to learn more. I specialize in web development using the MERN Stack. While taking on this facet of the campaign, I gained new tools that led me to a digital fundraising firm where I worked for 2 years.

When I'm not coding, you can find me either on my bike, in the mountains hiking, or watching the Buffalo Bills (probably lose) on Sunday.
