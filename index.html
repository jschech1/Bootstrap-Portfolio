About Me

Tech: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), Handlebars, MySQL, Sequelize, Bootstrap, Heroku, Firebase, MAMP, Git, Postman, VS Code, Sublime, Google Developer Tools, Data Visualization, Qvidian, WordPress, Squarespace, Shopify, Blue State Digital

I am a highly disciplined and goal oriented change maker with a range of experience in full stack web development, digital marketing, data analytics, and teamwork. I am a critical thinker and I have a constant itch to learn more. I specialize in web development using the MERN Stack. I thrive off of utilizing data and technology to create beautiful and practical web pages.

I received a BA at West Virginia University in Political Science and my Masters of Public Administration from American University. From there, I spent four years on the campaign trail, working for Congressional and Mayoral campaigns in the field. While working on these campaigns, I fell in love with running each digital program. While taking on this facet of the campaign, I gained new tools that led me to a digital fundraising firm where I worked for two years. And that's what led to my interest and pursuit toward a career in web development at the University of Denver's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp!

When I'm not coding, you can find me either on my bike, in the mountains hiking, or watching the Buffalo Bills (probably lose) on Sunday.
